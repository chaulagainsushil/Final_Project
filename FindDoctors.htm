Our Departments

Department of Cardiology

Cardiologists in this department specialize in conditions like coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and congenital heart defects. They use advanced techniques, including electrocardiograms (ECGs), echocardiograms, stress tests, and cardiac catheterizations, to assess heart health and recommend the best treatment options.

Department of Neurology

Neurologists in this department address a wide range of conditions, including stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, Alzheimer's disease, migraines, and other neurological disorders. They use advanced diagnostic tools such as MRIs, CT scans, EEGs, and EMGs to evaluate brain and nerve function.

Department of Orthopedics

The Department of Orthopedics specializes in diagnosing and treating conditions related to the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, and muscles. Orthopedic doctors address fractures, arthritis, sports injuries, and spine disorders, offering treatments like joint replacement and minimally invasive surgeries. The goal is to restore mobility and relieve pain, helping patients improve their quality of life.
