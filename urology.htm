Dr. Bijay Mansingh Katwal

Dr. Katwal is a highly experienced urologist and kidney transplant surgeon. He is specialized in managing prostate health, urinary stone disease, and urological cancers, including those of the kidney, prostate, bladder, and testis. He works at Sumeru Hospital, Dhapakhel, and has pioneered laparoscopic kidney transplant surgeries in Nepal. He also has expertise in laparoscopic and robotic surgerie.​

Dr. Mahesh Bahadur Adhikari

Serving as the Principal Consultant and Head of Urology at Nepal Mediciti, Dr. Adhikari is a seasoned urologist with over 20 years of experience. He specializes in urinary stone disease, prostate health, and uro-oncology. Dr. Adhikari has contributed significantly to the field of endourology and has trained many urologists across Nepal.

Dr. Jit Prakash Shrestha

A consultant urologist and andrologist with extensive experience. He provides consultations both in-person and online. His schedule is from Sunday to Friday starting at 2 PM. He has worked in various hospitals and clinics across Nepal and offers specialized care in urology and andrology​ ​

Dr. Prashant Mishra

Consultant in urology with a specialization in urooncology. He holds an MBBS and MS in Urology and provides consultation on an on-call basis. Dr. Mishra is highly regarded for his expertise in treating urological conditions including cancers of the urinary system.
